# Training Plan

## Overview

This training plan provides a systematic and consistent approach to train the trainees at different levels ensuring they are digitally adept with the usage of Salama platform (DIGIT HCM application) and its various modules and functionalities. It has been created to address the need of training the teams on the ground before the go-live of the group 4 campaign in Tete province of Mozambique.

The trainings be performed at the following levels:&#x20;

* Master Trainer Training
* Central ToT
* Provincial ToT
* Local monitor-level training
* Registrar-level training

## Participants

The following are the key participants/stakeholders for various levels of trainings:&#x20;

* National-level supervisors&#x20;
* Provincial-level supervisors
* District-level supervisors
* Warehouse manager
* Local monitors (ML)
* Registrars (from the distribution team) (RD)
* Helpdesk staff
* Any other nominated members from NMCP, DIS, DTIC, WV and partnering entities.
